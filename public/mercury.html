☿ Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest in the solar system. Despite its proximity to the Sun, it's not the hottest planet.

With no atmosphere to retain heat, Mercury’s surface temperature swings from extremely hot to freezing. Its surface is heavily cratered and resembles our Moon.

Mercury has no moons and completes its orbit around the Sun in just 88 Earth days, making it the fastest planet in our solar system.
