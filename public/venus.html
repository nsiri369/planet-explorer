♀ Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is similar in size and structure to Earth. However, it has a thick, toxic atmosphere that traps heat, making it the hottest planet in the solar system.

Its surface is covered with volcanoes and plains. Venus rotates very slowly and in the opposite direction of most planets.

A day on Venus is longer than its year. It’s known for its bright appearance and is often called Earth’s “sister planet.”
